Manchurian

The dish is typically characterized by its bold flavors, which come from ingredients like garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and a blend of spices.tossed in a savory and tangy sauce.

Pasta

Pasta is a versatile and beloved Italian dish made from durum wheat flour and water, typically formed into various shapes and sizes. It can be enjoyed fresh or dried and is a staple in many cuisines around the world.

Momos

Momos are a popular type of steamed dumpling originating from Tibet and widely enjoyed in various regions of India, especially in the northeastern states. They are made from a simple dough of flour and water, which is rolled out into thin circles and filled with a variety of ingredients.
