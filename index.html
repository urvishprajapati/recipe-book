Manchurian

Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with deep-fried vegetable or meat balls. It's often served with a tangy, flavorful sauce made of soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, giving it a bold and savory taste.

Ingredients: Flour, Soy Sauce, Garlic, Ginger, Green Onions, Corn Flour

1. Mix the ingredients for the balls. 2. Fry and set aside. 3. Prepare the sauce. 4. Toss the fried balls in the sauce.

Momos

Momos are a type of steamed dumpling, originating from Tibet and popular in many parts of India. They are often filled with vegetables, chicken, or pork, and served with spicy dipping sauces.

Ingredients: Flour, Ginger, Garlic, Cabbage, Carrot, Soy Sauce

1. Prepare the dough. 2. Make the filling. 3. Steam the dumplings.
